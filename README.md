# LiftOff

# Product Description
LiftOff is a mobile application to make gym workouts more accessible and engaging for users. Our app aims to lower the barrier of entry to gym routines by simplifying workout planning and tracking, with a social aspect to engage with and compete with your friends. It addresses common challenges gym-goers face, such as creating personalized workout plans, staying motivated, and organizing workout schedules.Â
